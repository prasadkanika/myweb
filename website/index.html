1st Hard disk


In September 1956, the first 5MB hard disk unit weighed over a ton and was used by the IBM 305 RAMAC, which was the first commercial computer to have a moving-head hard disk. The drive occupied 16 square feet and used fifty 24-inch platters read by two independently moving arms that had an average access time of 600ms.


The Firefox logo isn’t a fox.


The animal on the logo is not a fox ,it's a red panda, a rare and protected species that are only found in Asia. On the Mozilla logo, the panda is embracing the blue globe. The meaning behind this design choice is that the browser works fast and is accessible to users worldwide.

Apple's 1st logo
